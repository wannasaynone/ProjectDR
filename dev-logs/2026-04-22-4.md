### 步驟 6：B4f DialogueFlowInstaller 新建

**新檔案**：`Assets/Game/Scripts/Village/Core/Manager/DialogueFlowInstaller.cs`

- 實作 `IVillageInstaller` + `IVillageTickable`
- 建構子接受：`CharacterQuestionsConfigData`、`GreetingConfigData`、`IdleChatConfigData`、`AffinityManager`、`RedDotManager`（允許 null）、`float characterQuestionCountdownSeconds`、`float dialogueCooldownBaseSeconds`
- Install：建構 7 個 Manager（CharacterQuestionsConfig、CharacterQuestionsManager、CharacterQuestionCountdownManager、GreetingConfig、GreetingPresenter、IdleChatConfig、IdleChatPresenter）+ DialogueCooldownManager + CharacterStaminaManager；訂閱 3 個事件（CommissionStartedEvent、CommissionClaimedEvent、CharacterUnlockedEvent）
- Uninstall：解除 3 個事件訂閱；Dispose CountdownManager + CooldownManager
- Tick：驅動 CharacterQuestionCountdownManager + DialogueCooldownManager
- 公開 Accessor：`CharacterQuestionsManager`、`CharacterQuestionCountdownManager`、`GreetingPresenter`、`IdleChatPresenter`、`DialogueCooldownManager`、`StaminaManager`

**關鍵技術決策**：
- `AffinityManager` 不實作 `IAffinityQuery`，無法透過 `ctx.AffinityReadOnly` downcast 取得。改為建構子直接注入 `AffinityManager`（暫時路徑）。
- TODO 標記：待 AffinityInstaller 完成後，將 AffinityManager 注入路徑改由 VillageContext 取得。
